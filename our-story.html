Our Story

Solar4Ever is ten years old in April 2021. I’m proud of that. My father Barry, “the fastest sparkie in the West” and his father, George, were well respected Perth electricians , so it was no huge surprise that I ended up making a living from electricity.

Ten years is a long time for solar companies who generally don’t last more than three years. It’s a difficult industry for many reasons. We’ve seen so many come and go. Our team achieved that ten year milestone by being consistent. Now for the next ten.

No wild promises, no harassing sales reps and no corner-cutting on safe electrical installation work. Our team strive to be always helpful, and most of all, professional.

Call us first if you need help to get your head around solar; oh yes SO many options, or call us last if you know what you want and want to ‘seal the deal’ on budget, mid-range or premium solar equipment with a company that’s local and here to stay.

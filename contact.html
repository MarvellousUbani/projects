Sales Enquiries, Quotes

If you have any questions, just click the CHAT button bottom right.

If you would like a quotation for a new solar installation then please send an email to info@solar4ever.com.au with the following information:

Existing homes and businesses

  1. The installation address (we can not quote at all without this)
  2. A picture of your switchboard (optional but very helpful)
  3. Your name, phone number and email address
  4. If already have a good idea of what you want, let us know.
  5. If you know what your typical Synergy bill is, then let us know that.

New homes (not completed yet)

If your house is not built yet, then we can not quote until it is completed.

If it is a double storey home you are building then make sure your builder runs a 25mm heavy duty solar labelled conduit (available from electrical solar wholesalers like MM Electrical) with a draw wire inside from the roof to the inverter location.
